Focaccia with Caramelized Onions Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetitzer topped with sweet caramellized onions, tart ceanberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favourite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/ cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus mor for the pan

For the focaccia

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thiny sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scarpe rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not neccessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Metod

  1. make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
